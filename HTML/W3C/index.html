About W3C

The World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) is an international community that develops open standards to ensure the long-term growth of the Web.

W3C Standards

W3C develops standards for the Web in areas such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and many others. These standards are designed to promote consistency and interoperability across different web technologies.

W3C Members

W3C's membership includes a diverse range of organizations and individuals who contribute to the development of web standards. Members include technology companies, research institutions, governments, and more.

Contact W3C

If you have any questions or would like to get involved with W3C, you can contact us through our website or via email.
